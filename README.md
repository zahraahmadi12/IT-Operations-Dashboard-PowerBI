# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard: Transforming Workforce Data into Insights

## 🌟 The Story Behind the Project

Every organization's greatest asset is its people. But why do some employees leave while others stay?

This project started with an Excel dataset containing 1,470 employee records. The challenge was not just to show numbers, but to uncover the human story behind the data by identifying patterns of attrition that can help HR departments build better retention strategies.

## 🚀 What I Built

I developed a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard that provides a clear view of employee dynamics. This is not just a visualization project; it is a diagnostic tool designed to bridge the gap between raw data and business decision-making.

## 🛠️ Technical Deep Dive

I used a self-service BI approach, keeping the data preparation, modeling, and reporting process inside the Power BI ecosystem for better transparency and maintainability.

### Power Query

Instead of relying on external preprocessing, I prepared and cleaned the data directly in Power BI. This included:

- Checking data quality and consistency
- Handling missing or null values
- Optimizing data types for analysis
- Preparing the dataset for dashboard reporting

### DAX & Feature Engineering

I created custom DAX measures and calculated fields to add analytical depth to the dashboard, including:

- Dynamic Attrition Rate
- Age Grouping
- Employee Status
- Job Satisfaction Analysis

### Dashboard Design & UI/UX

I designed a clean, high-contrast dashboard layout focused on readability and usability. The dashboard includes interactive slicers and cross-filtering features, allowing users to drill down into specific areas such as education field, department, age group, gender, and job role.

## 📈 Key Insights & Results

- Attrition Reality: Identified a 16.12% overall attrition rate.
- Workforce Segmentation: Analyzed attrition patterns across age groups, gender, departments, education fields, and job roles.
- Job Satisfaction Analysis: Visualized job satisfaction ratings across different job roles to support deeper HR investigation.
- Business Value: The dashboard helps HR teams identify potential risk areas and make more data-driven retention decisions.
